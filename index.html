Educational Purpose

Inform and Transform Society

The term "educational" is defined by 26 CFR §1.501(c)(3)-1 to encompass both
(1) individual instruction “for the purpose of improving or developing [individual]
capabilities,” and/or (2) “the instruction of the public on subjects useful to the
individual and beneficial to the community.”

Participants in Voluntary Service Programs
